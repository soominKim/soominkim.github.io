This is Soomin Kim😎

I am an HCI and UX researcher at Samsung Electronics MX (Mobile Experience) division. I am also the author of the bestseller The Great Transformation of ChatGPT (ì±—GPT ê±°ëŒ€í•œ ì „í™˜).

I recently gave a talk at Samsung SSDC, titled "Developing User and Interaction-Centered AI Services", sharing insights on how to design AI systems that enhance user agency and experience.

My research bridges social science, interaction design, and AI, with a focus on building human-centered AI systems. I design AI-infused experiences that turn user insights into tangible interventions â€” from prompt engineering to system-level prototyping.

Recently, I have been exploring how generative AI can enhance user creativity and productivity in everyday tasks. This includes PromptPilot (IJHCI 2025), a system that recommends context-appropriate prompts based on task type and user input. I also examined user experiences with AI image generation systems, highlighting how prompt refinement and system feedback shape creativity (IJHCI 2024).

Previously, I specialized in conversational agents, developing a high-quality data collection system (CHI 2019 full paper) and designing the group chat facilitator agent Poki (CHI 2020 full paper, CSCW 2021 full paper), which enabled structured interaction in group discussions. My broader interests lie in Human-AI Interaction, generative UX, and designing interactive systems that support user creativity, initiative, and meaningful collaboration with AI.
